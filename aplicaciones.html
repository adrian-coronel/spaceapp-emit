Snow Properties Study

What is Albedo?

Albedo is the fraction of light that a surface reflects. The albedo of the Earth's surface (atmosphere, ocean, land surfaces) determines how much incoming solar energy, or light, is immediately reflected back into space. This can have an impact on the climate.

Forest Management and Fires

What Can EMIT Do?

Forest lands, both in the western US and worldwide, face an increasing risk of mortality due to droughts, beetle outbreaks, and forest fires.

EMIT data can be used after a forest fire to assess recovery and monitor current risks.
